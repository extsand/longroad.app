About Us

We are a small development team who really love travel. Once we went on a hike, and walked many kilometers. On our way, we met many wonderful animals and amazing plants. Our trip has brought us many new impressions and pleasant memories.

This trip inspired us to create this awesome app where you can find all the information you need to have a deeper travel experience and enjoyment with your friends, colleagues or family. Open up new horizons, mark interesting places, share your impressions with your friends. Bicycle, motorcycle, walking or long road trip - all of these are a wonderful addition to our amazing travel app.

it doesn't matter what city or country you are from, how old you are, or what your occupation. E veryone will find something of their own during the journey. Let this be the best and most unforgettable experience and delightful adventure for you.
